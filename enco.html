Overview

For a University Project, I was tasked with designing a brand strategy and concept for a studio collective which valued sustainability. Over the course of this project I used Adobe Illustrator to create a logo, colour scheme, typography system, and visual language. Part of the deliverables included a pitch deck in which my team were required to pitch 3 high-level concepts to address waste management in Australia. I created a presentation which utilised these brand guidelines to generate visuals aided in communication.
