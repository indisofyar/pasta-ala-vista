Graphic Design

Enco Studio Concept

Role
Team Lead and Designer
Length
5 Weeks
Purpose
University Assignment
Software
Adobe Illustrator

Overview

For a University Project, I was tasked with designing a brand strategy and concept for a studio collective which valued sustainability. Over the course of this project I used Adobe Illustrator to create a logo, colour scheme, typography system, and visual system. Part of the deliverables included a pitch deck in which my team were required to pitch 3 high-level concepts to address waste management in Australia. I created a presentation which utilised these brand guidelines to generate visuals aided in communication.

Development

During the development I performed a high degree of sketching and iterating to explore different ideas of what the logo and the brand might look and feel like. I explored a range of different logo possibilities keeping in mind that I aimed to produce something which was both simple and responsive as well as was rooted in ideas of sustainability. I came up with the name "Enco" which stood for Encompass. I thought that Encompass could perhaps be a metaphor for a larger thing such a hug or the universe. I also explored an alternative name "The Sustainability Movement" (see below).

Outcomes

As a result of this iteration, I was able develop a series of assets which connected to eachother and had similar visual language. This aided my team in communicating our final presentation and assisted us in attaining a high distinction for this project.
